Writing in Node.js has its pros and cons. Among the pros, you can list low development times, the biggest community in the world and easier hiring due to the popularity of the language, but I believe it is just as important to take the cons into consideration while choosing a job/language/framework and bascially almost everything in life. In this post, I will show why Javascript's cons got to me and why I decided to switch to Elixir after learning the stdlib and writing in it for a few months.
